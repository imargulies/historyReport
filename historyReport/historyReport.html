History Report

The history report Add-In is a straight forward way to look at your vehicle's location history. To run the report select a vehicle from the options menu. The interval you select determines how often to look up the vehicle's location. If the vehicle you are looking for is not shown in the list, select the "Show historical vehicles" check box as the vehicle may have been archived when moving the Geotab GO Device between vehicles.
